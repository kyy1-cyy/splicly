Welcome to Splicy

¶ What are we?

We are a community that hosts VR paid games for free. Unlike VRPirates, this is completely standalone, meaning you can head to our Discord, request your game, we'll add it, and you can download it.

We do in fact get all our games from VRPirates - check them out!
